# Saudi Deal Finder - Pricing Strategy

## Executive Summary

Saudi Deal Finder positions itself as an **investment intelligence tool** rather than a listing site. The value proposition centers on saving investors time and money by surfacing undervalued opportunities before the broader market.

## Subscription Tiers

### Free Tier (Lead Generation)
**Purpose:** Hook users, demonstrate value, drive conversions

**Includes:**
- Top 3 deals per day (highest investment score)
- Basic filters: City, Property Type
- 24-hour delayed data
- No contact info for sellers
- View-only mode (no saves, no alerts)
- Watermarked deal analysis

**Conversion Triggers:**
- "Unlock 47 more deals in Riyadh" counters
- Blurred contact info with upgrade prompt
- "See price history" locked features
- Email drip: "You missed 12 hot deals this week"

---

### Premium Tier - $29/month
**Target:** Active individual investors, first-time buyers

**Includes:**
- Unlimited deal access
- Real-time data (updated every 4 hours)
- Advanced filters:
  - Price range
  - Price per m²
  - Investment score threshold
  - Days on market
  - Price drop percentage
- Email alerts for new deals matching saved searches
- Price history charts (30/60/90 days)
- Save favorites & notes
- Export to CSV
- Basic support (48hr response)
- Mobile app access

**Value Metrics:**
- Average user finds 2-3 deals/month worth investigating
- Average saved per deal found: 50,000-150,000 SAR
- ROI: 1,700x - 5,000x monthly subscription cost

---

### Pro Tier - $99/month
**Target:** Professional investors, real estate agencies, developers

**Includes (Everything in Premium +):**
- Instant Telegram/WhatsApp alerts (< 5 min delay)
- API access (1,000 requests/day)
- Webhook notifications
- Bulk export & data feeds
- Priority support (4hr response)
- Personal deal consultant (1hr/month strategy call)
- Off-market deals network (user-submitted opportunities)
- Team accounts (up to 5 users)
- White-label reports

**Value Metrics:**
- Speed advantage: 4-hour head start on all deals
- API enables integration with CRM/portfolio management
- Off-market deals: 15-20% of users report closing these

---

## Enterprise / Custom

**Pricing:** $500+/month (custom quote)

**Includes:**

## Pricing Psychology

### Anchoring Strategy
- Show "Retail Price" crossed out: Premium $49 → $29, Pro $199 → $99
- Frame as "Beta Pricing" or "Founding Member Rate"
- Annual discount: 2 months free (17% savings)

### Regional Considerations
- Prices in USD for international investors
- SAR equivalent shown prominently
- Accept both currencies via Stripe
- Plan for STC Pay, Mada integration for local market

### Trial Strategy
- 7-day free trial for Premium
- No credit card required
- Full access during trial
- Hard paywall after day 7

- Server costs: ~$500/month (DigitalOcean/AWS)

## Promotional Strategies

### Launch Offers
- First 100 subscribers: 50% off first 3 months
- Referral program: 1 month free for each paying referral
- Real estate influencer partnerships (commission-based)

### Retention Tactics
- Monthly "Deal Report" showing value delivered
- Gamification: Badges for active users
- Community: Private Telegram group for Pro users
- Annual subscribers get exclusive market reports

### Upsell Path
1. Free user signs up
2. Day 3: Email with "hidden" deal they can't access
3. Day 7: Trial offer email
4. Day 10: Case study: "How Ahmed saved 200K SAR"
5. Day 14: Limited-time discount offer
6. Monthly: "You missed X deals this month"
